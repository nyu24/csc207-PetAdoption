# Team Contract

---
## Purpose of this Contract

This contract sets out shared expectations and commitments for how our team will collaborate during the course. It is designed to promote accountability, professionalism, and mutual support as we complete individual preparation, readiness assurance tests, in-class activities, and our course project.

---
## Team Norms and Expectations

### Communication
* Discord and Instagram will both be used for communication outside of class.
* Each teammate should take at maximum 2 days (or before the nearest deadline) to respond to a question.
* If you can't make a deadline on time, if you can't make it to lecture or lab. If something has gone wrong, please contact the team.
* Active listening, in general be respectful of your peers, allow for constructive criticism, don't hesitate to speak out if you feel it's necessary (respectfully). 
---

### Other expectations
 - Put in effort in your work, don't procrastinate.
 - Don't use AI without consulting the team.

---

## Decision Making
* Mainly through majority consensus.

---
## Conflict resolution
* Listening to each other's side of the issue and attempting to reach a compromise.
* Bring the issue to the team, if unable to come to an agreement.

---

## Accountability

* Complete the work in a timely manner.
* If something has gone wrong, communicate it to the team, don't stay silent.
